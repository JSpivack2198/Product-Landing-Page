About the Creators of Lagrange 00

The Lagrange 00 is a group of like minded individuals that create public content for our viewers and Gunpla Building Community. Almost all of us either stream on Twitch or submit our pieces through our Discords for everyone to enjoy. we also take on commission work upon request and those that we do on our own can sometimes go up for sales,auctions or even for charity events. Currently involoved with our group are our main builders GeekinMiami, Carbondobs, and SpartanTypeZero. Each of us tends to use a different building style as we have some of our works sampled below for sale. If you ever have any questions please reach out to us from here or use the links provided below.
